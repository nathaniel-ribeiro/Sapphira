# Sapphira - Anti-Cheat for Online Xiangqi

## Overview
This project bring the seminal work of Ken Regan on anti-cheat for chess to the game of Xiangqi. Note that this
repository is a work-in-progress.

Where does the name come from?
Ananias and Sapphira were a couple from the early Christian church who were struck down for deceiving the Holy Spirit
regarding the sale of a plot of land. Similarly, this software seeks to "strike down" duplicitous Xiangqi players who 
consult engines or strong players during their games.
## Features
* Detects elevator/alt accounts
* Platform-agnostic
* Compatible with your favorite UCI-compliant pikafish
